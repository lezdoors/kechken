# Maison Tanneurs Photography Reference Register

Updated 2026-05-28. This replaces the historical Kechken apparel photography
brief. Maison Tanneurs launch scope is bags and small leather goods only.

## North Star

Quiet French luxury leather goods with a Marrakech craft origin. The imagery
should feel restrained, expensive, material-led, and globally credible.

Reference the calm of Polene, Le Tanneur, Lemaire, Loewe, and Aether-style
cinematic full-bleed sections, but translate those references into Maison
Tanneurs' own vocabulary: full-grain cognac leather, brass hardware, atelier
hands, limestone architecture, courtyard light, and clean product photography.

## In / Out

| In | Out |
| --- | --- |
| Full-bleed cinematic hero frames | Generic Shopify product banners |
| Clean white or limestone PDP backgrounds | Raw supplier screenshots |
| Deep cognac, tobacco, espresso leather tones | Saturated novelty colors |
| Brushed brass details, grain, edge-paint, stitching | Fake logos or unreadable text |
| Courtyard, atelier, stone, shadow, window light | Souk/bazaar tourism cliches |
| Calm model styling that supports the bag | Apparel-first fashion campaign |
| One bag as the clear subject | Collages, grids, multi-product confusion |

## Product Media Rules

1. Use HF-improved or confirmed Drive multishot exports only.
2. Raw screenshots, Oussam uploads, supplier originals, and screenshot folders
   are reference-only. Do not encode, upload, or wire them into PDP galleries.
3. Every accepted product set should be renamed:
   `<product-slug>-pdp-01.webp` through `<product-slug>-pdp-09.webp`.
4. Product identity beats filename. Match hardware, strap count, pocket count,
   stitching, closure, silhouette, and color before upload.
5. Reject collage/contact-sheet outputs, changed product geometry, fake brand
   marks, wrong background, and obvious AI artifacts.

## Required 9-Shot Mix

- Front hero
- Rear
- Left side
- Right side
- Front three-quarter
- Rear three-quarter
- Interior/top detail
- Hardware/stitching macro
- Strap/handle construction detail

## Hero And Editorial Rules

- Use cinematic lifestyle/editorial imagery for hero and story sections, not
  plain PDP product shots.
- Product cards and PDP galleries should stay clean, white, quiet, and focused.
- Moroccan influence should come through architecture, light, leather craft,
  texture, and material provenance, not decorative motifs.
- No clothing, jackets, outerwear, or apparel campaign prompts for this launch.

## Aspect Ratios

| Surface | Ratio |
| --- | --- |
| Homepage hero | 16:9 landscape or 9:16/4:5 crop-safe source |
| Product card | 4:5 portrait |
| PDP main image | 4:5 portrait |
| PDP detail crops | 1:1 or 4:5 |
| Social/editorial | 4:5 portrait or 9:16 vertical |
